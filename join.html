What is the best level for your business?

Non-Profit

This level is for non-profit businesses only. You recieve the same benefits as a bronze business at half the cost! Additionally will place a symbol on your ads letting everyone know that you are a non-profit organization.

Bronze

This membership level of the Chamber of Commerce offers multiple benefits. Among those benefits are:

  • Ads in both the online directory and a printed directory.
  • Acess to "After Hours Mixers" for networking.
  • Membership also adds legitimacy to your business.

Silver

This membership level of the Chamber of Commerce offers multiple benefits in addition to all the benefits of the bronze membership level, it also offers the following benefits:

  • Free newsletter advertising
  • Member to member discounts

Gold

This membership level of the Chamber of Commerce offers multiple benefits in addition to all the benefits of the silver and bronze membership levels, it also offers the following benefits:

  • A "Featured Member" on the website homepage
  • 5% discount on Chamber events
  • A set of 600 membership mailing labels for mailers
  • Insurance discounts for your business
  • Affordable payroll assistance
  • Reduced electricity costs
